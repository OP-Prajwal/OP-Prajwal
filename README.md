## <img src="https://media.giphy.com/media/hvRJCLFzcasrR4ia7z/giphy.gif" width="28"/> About Me

hey, I'm Prajwal — a dev from India who loves building things that actually work. I mostly live at the crossroads of full-stack and AI, whether that's wiring up a RAG pipeline, training a deepfake detector, or shipping a web app end-to-end.

- currently deep into **multimodal AI** and **video deepfake detection** — research meets production
- spend a lot of time building agentic systems with **LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI** — the orchestration layer is where it gets interesting
- love **system-level programming** — understanding what's happening under the hood matters to me, not just what the API returns
- write **Rust** when I want things fast and correct, **Python** when I want things done
- actively **freelancing** — if you've got an AI or full-stack problem worth solving, let's talk
- I don't just use tools, I try to understand why they work
